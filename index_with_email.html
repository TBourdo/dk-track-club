What We Are

DK Track Club is an independent indoor track and field club unaffiliated with the MHSAA or Delton-Kellogg Schools. We focus on developing running fundamentals and skills for kids of all ages across southwest Michigan.

Meet the Coach

Tyler Bourdo is a 2011 graduate of Delton-Kellogg Schools, where he competed in every event from the 100-meter dash to the 5,000-meter run in cross country. He continued his athletic career at Trine University, earning All-American honors four times while competing at the national level. Today, Tyler volunteers his time to lead DK Track Club, providing opportunities for student-athletes in southwest Michigan to develop their running fundamentals and skills.
